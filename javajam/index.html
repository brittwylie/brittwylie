Java Jam's Story

Java Jam began with humble beginnings. A lonely soul made his coffee day in and day out. It was more than just a daily routine for him. It was an art. Fancy leaf lattes and delicious, rich, dark coffee. Although his life was filled with coffee, it was lacking something. “Why bother making coffee if I’m the only one who can enjoy it?” he thought. “It’s time to make some phone calls.” Sunday morning rolled around and the people came rolling in to his small, single bedroom apartment. “This coffee is fantastic!” exclaimed his friends. “Wow! Look at that fancy leaf!” He was a success. After several weeks of free coffee parties, another idea popped into his head. Across the street, a small little hole in the wall was vacant. A few permits and coffee makers later, Java Jam was born!
