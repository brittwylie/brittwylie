Events

Java Jam is known for being the late night chill spot after a long day at work. One of the most popular days to visit is our Two Dollar Tuesday’s. Every Tuesday all beverages including alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages are sold at the price of only $2. On this day, people are able to join a great night at the café, with just a small fee. Java Jam makes sure to always have something going on including Poetry Night, Open Mic Night, Comedy Night, and days with featured guests and band performances. If you would like to sign up for any of these nights, please feel free to visit and fill out our contact page, listing what you would like to do in the comments area. There are also cool holiday events this month, please come join us to celebrate!

Please visit our contact page for Sign-ups.
