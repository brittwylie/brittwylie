About Me

Hello! My name is Britt. I am a recent graduate of The University of Texas at Arlington with a major in Communication Technology and will be graduating in May of 2017. I have a passion for creating user experiences that are dynamic, unique, and responsive and enhance customer engagement with the brand.

I have been designing websites as a hobby since high school and decided to pursue it as a profession during my sophomore year of college. Before I chose web development and internet marketing as my career path I was focused on pursuing a career in photography.

In my personal life I enjoy photography, graphic design, and web design as hobbies. In addition to these creative pursuits I enjoy running 5Ks, hiking, reading, and listening to podcasts.
